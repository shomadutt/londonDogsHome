About us

We are one of the leading re-homing centres in the UK. Every year we rescue, rehabilitate and re-home thousands of dogs, but we're still full. Dogs come to us for all sorts of reasons.

People can't afford to keep them, their owner died, they've strayed or not been claimed. Some are subjected to cruelty and neglect and some are just not wanted.

Every year we give dogs a second chance. To be able to continue, we need your support.
