Our Mission

The purpose of this endeavor is to aid unfortunate dogs in the interest of a higher quality of life. We take in rescued, confiscated, neglected, injured, ill, unmanageable, or otherwise unwanted dogs from private owners, zoos, shelters, and other public organisations. These dogs are cared for, and/or rehabilitated to the best of our ability and means until which time they can be found healthy, happy homes.

The Rescue

We are a community-based organisation; we welcome you to spend some time at our facility. We are a family-friendly establishment focused on educating pet owners and animal lovers about the needs and responsibilities of pet ownership as well as behaviour and training issues and health and wellbeing concerns. Neighbourhood children can volunteer to help take care of the dogs, and learn compassion as well as proper feeding, grooming, and handling.
