News

We send monthly enewsletters which gives you a peek behind-the-scenes at the Home, keeps you up-to-date with the re-homing numbers, and of course includes photos of some of the gorgeous dogs that your support helps keep safe. We will also send you event, volunteering and fundraising news when we have it throughout the year.

Our paper newsletter is sent in April and November each year. It has updates about the work of the Home, animal stories, details about our appeals and raffle tickets if you want to take part.
